# vi

- **Cursor movement**—`h, j, k, l` (left, down, up, and right)
- **Delete character**—`x`
- **Delete line**—`dd`
- **Mode toggle**—`Esc`, `Insert` (or `i`)
- **Quit**—`:q`
- **Quit without saving**—`:q!`
- **Run a shell command**—`:sh` (use `'exit'` to return)
- **Save file**—`:w`
- **Text search**—`/`
